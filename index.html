Joshua Malemba

Meet Joshua Malemba, founder of JMServices, first class software engineering undergraduate and full time software developer on placement at Uniper. During his first two years at university, Joshua quickly came to discover the various avenues of technology and how they are spearheading the future, whether that be globally, institutionally, and locally. However, marked by the sparsity of ethnic minority involvement, he was inspired to try make change. As a consequence, he initiated this service to help educate younger ethnic minorities in local schools/colleges on involving themselves in the space, hoping to make a difference and see a more diverse range of young creative ethnic talent challenging the stigma and bringing new dynamics to the industry.
